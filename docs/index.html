👋 Before we begin

Language is beautifully messy. Computers hate that.

Humans understand jokes, sarcasm, emotions, idioms, spelling mistakes, incomplete sentences — even silence. Computers don't. This course is about closing that gap, one algorithm at a time.

Over the next seven units, you'll teach machines to read, understand, analyse, and generate human language — the same technology quietly running inside ChatGPT, Google Translate, Grammarly, Alexa, Siri, and every search engine you've ever yelled at.

No prior NLP experience required. Just bring curiosity — the machine will supply the confusion.
